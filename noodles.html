Everest noodle house is a well known noodle bar in the sadderlers center.Founded in 2014 its grown to become the noodle bar if your craving some freash greasy dare i say sloppy noodles. Everest has a dish that caters to anyones needs offering a wide range of differernt flavors and spices from spice kings favorite naga noodle to wimpy white girls (joe) everest box, coming in a convient box its also a great meal on the go if your in a rush. Everest does not only sell noodles but also a wide range of other foods, like frozen indo burgers, soup, currys and many more.
