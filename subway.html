Subway is a well-known international fast-food franchise that specializes in made-to-order submarine sandwiches and salads. Founded in 1965, Subway has since grown into one of the largest restaurant chains in the world, with thousands of locations across numerous countries. The brand's unique selling point is its "build-your-own" concept, allowing customers to customize their sandwiches by choosing from a variety of freshly baked bread, toppings, condiments, and fillings. Subway offers a wide range of options to accommodate different dietary preferences, including vegetarian and healthier choices. Beyond sandwiches, the menu also includes wraps, breakfast items, soups, and a selection of sides and beverages. With its focus on offering healthier alternatives, Subway became known for its marketing slogan "Eat Fresh." The franchise operates on a fast-food model, providing quick service and the option for customers to dine in or take their orders to go. Subway's prominence and success lie in its commitment to providing fresh ingredients, customizable options, and convenient meal solutions for people on the go.
